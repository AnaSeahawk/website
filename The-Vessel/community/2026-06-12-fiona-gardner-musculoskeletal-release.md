## Contributor Account

Fiona reported a notable change after approximately six to seven weeks of working with distilled plasma.

For over a year she had experienced significant shoulder pain, particularly at night. During the weeks after introducing distilled plasma, she observed the gradual disappearance of that pain alongside other systemic changes she described as difficult to fully articulate.

She did not describe the change only as symptom relief. She described a broader shift in the body's internal quality: increased fluidity, reduced heaviness, and a gradual release of tension held deep within the tissues.

In her own interpretation, she wonders whether distilled plasma may carry a greater magnetic potential than its source material. This remains her personal hypothesis, not a measured conclusion. The language itself is worth preserving: strengthening the body's field, supporting the nervous system, and restoring movement through what she calls the body's waterways.

She repeatedly observed that distilled plasma feels different from ordinary hydration. She characterized it as a fluid that carries less burden, having already passed through a cycle of biological processing and refinement. In her experience, this appears to support a deeper form of hydration associated with ease, lubrication, and the release of longstanding restriction.
