# Living Ferments

*Kitchen alchemy: the practice of fermentation as earth care, body care, and nourishment.*

This folder holds two related practices — water alchemy and soil alchemy — that began as workshop content in 2018 and have continued to evolve through direct use.

They are not separate practices. The water ferment produces liquid and solids. The solids go into the soil ferment. The soil feeds the food that feeds the ferment. The loop closes.
