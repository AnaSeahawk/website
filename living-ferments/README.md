# Living Ferments

*Kitchen alchemy: the practice of fermentation as earth care, body care, and nourishment.*

This folder holds four related practices — water alchemy, body alchemy, soil alchemy, and kefir — that began as workshop content in 2018 and have continued to evolve through direct use.

They are not separate practices. The water ferment produces liquid and solids. The solids go into the soil ferment. The whey from kefir starts the soil system and the body care ferments. The fermented tonic goes inward. The amended soil feeds the food that feeds the ferment. The loop closes.
