# Observation Protocol
## Sovereign Biophysics — Living Waters Practice

*A field record template. Nothing here is required. The body teaches itself.*

---

## Before the Run

**Date / Sun / Moon phase** — record where you are in the solar and lunar cycle. The material carries this.

**Days aged** — how long since collection. Note if fermentation is active (smell, pressure, visible change).

**Diet notes** — anything significant in the preceding days: sodium, animal protein, specific plants, fasting, water source.

**Body state** — where you are in the monthly cycle if applicable. Energy, sleep, emotional weather. These are variables, not distractions.

---

## During the Run

**Start time / Heat applied**

**First fraction (Solar)** — when does character change? Note: smell, colour, behaviour at the condenser, volume collected.

**Second fraction (Lunar)** — the settling. Note: clarity, smell, how it differs from the first.

**Retentate (Saturn)** — colour, density, smell of what remains in the chamber.

**Temperature observations** — did anything unexpected rise? Colour in the condenser? Note it.

## After the Run

**Phenomenological notes** — what the body registered during and after working with the material. Taste, sensation, any shifts in state. This is data.

**Quality assessment** — how does this run compare to previous? What was different? Cleaner, heavier, faster, slower?

**What you notice over the following days** — appetite, sleep, skin, clarity, emotional tone. Not claims — observations.

---

## Over Time

A single run gives a reading. A month gives a pattern. A full year gives the range.

Track what shifts with the lunar cycle. Track what shifts with the season. Track what correlates with diet, sleep, emotional weather. The pattern is the data the single run cannot give you.
