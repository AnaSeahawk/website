# Oral Care Protocol
## The Two-Phase Application of Solar Spirit and Saturn Serum

*Single-practitioner record. Personal observation only. No clinical claims are made.*

---

The Solar fraction and the Saturn serum are chemically distinct substances that accomplish different things when applied to oral tissue. Used together in sequence — the spirit first, the serum second — they create conditions that neither could produce alone.

This protocol emerged from direct observation during the February 2026 distillation runs. It is specific to distilled, concentrated fractions. Raw, fresh fluid behaves differently and is addressed separately below under safety parameters.

---

## Why the Sequence Matters

The mouth is an acidic environment when bacteria are active. The bacteria that cause tooth decay and gum inflammation thrive in acid — they produce it as a metabolic byproduct, and the acid dissolves the mineral structure of the enamel over time.

The Solar fraction is alkaline. Its ammonia content — the volatile compound driven off first in any aged distillation — creates a sharp pH shift the moment it contacts the mouth. This alkaline environment does two things immediately: it halts acid-producing bacteria, and it opens the microscopic pores of the enamel. In an acidic environment, teeth lose minerals. In an alkaline environment, the enamel becomes receptive — the pores open, the mineral-absorbing capacity increases.

The Saturn serum carries the minerals. Phosphates, potassium, calcium, magnesium — the structural compounds of hydroxyapatite, the crystalline matrix of tooth enamel and bone. In a diet without sodium chloride, these are the dominant fixed salts remaining after distillation. There is no excess table salt to dominate the mineral profile, no harsh NaCl crystals to abrade the enamel or draw moisture from the gum tissue. What remains is the body's own structural chemistry, concentrated and delivered directly.

The sequencing is the point: the spirit prepares the surface. The serum delivers the building materials into the prepared surface. Used in reverse order, or combined, the mechanism is disrupted.

## Phase 1 — The Alkaline Preparation

**Material:** Solar fraction — the clear, alkaline, ammonia-carrying first cut of an aged distillation run.

**Application:** Take a small sip. Swish vigorously around the teeth and along the gumline for 30 to 60 seconds.

**What is happening:** The alkaline shift neutralises acid and lifts surface stains through the same mild bleaching action as the ammonia chemistry. The enamel pores open in response to the pH change, becoming receptive to mineral uptake. Bacteria in acid pockets at the gumline cannot survive the alkaline environment.

**Dilution:** If the spirit stings aggressively on contact, dilute slightly with pure water. A well-aged, clean-diet distillation is typically mild enough to use undiluted. The sting — if present — is the ammonia concentration; it should feel sharp but not burning.

**Disposal:** Spit this fraction out rather than swallowing. The alkaline load, if swallowed regularly, neutralises stomach acid and impairs digestion. The benefit of the spirit is topical — teeth and gums — not systemic. Its work is done in the mouth.

## Phase 2 — The Mineral Application

**Material:** Saturn serum — the filtered, concentrated retentate. Either the liquid 1/4-volume reduction stored in amber dropper bottles, or the paste from a further stovetop reduction. If crystallised, dissolve a small amount in a drop of the spirit immediately before use to create a smooth paste.

**Application:** Apply a small amount directly to the teeth and along the gumline using a soft brush or a clean finger. Allow it to dwell on the surface for one to two minutes before brushing gently. This is a restorative application, not a scrubbing one.

**What is happening:** The alkaline environment established in Phase 1 has opened the enamel's mineral absorption capacity. The serum delivers the phosphate, potassium, calcium, and magnesium matrix directly into that prepared surface. These are the exact compounds the enamel is built from — delivered in a bioavailable, concentrated form, in the optimal chemical environment for absorption.

**Caution on concentration:** If the serum has been reduced to near-crystalline state, work with it carefully. Hard mineral crystals can physically scratch enamel. The paste should be smooth. If it feels gritty, dissolve it further before applying.

## Safety Parameters

The three fractions are not interchangeable. Each has a different concentration profile and is appropriate for different tissue types.

**The Solar spirit and Saturn serum are for tooth enamel and tough external skin only.**

These two fractions are hyper-concentrated. They have been deliberately separated from the isotonic balance of the original fluid. They are not appropriate for the eyes, the nasal passage, or the inner ear.

- **Eyes:** The alkalinity of the Solar spirit causes liquefactive damage to corneal tissue. The mineral concentration of the Saturn serum causes osmotic shock. Neither is safe for ocular application.
- **Nose:** The ammonia vapour of the spirit inflames the olfactory receptors and sinus lining. The serum density clogs and irritates the nasal cilia.
- **Ears:** Both the spirit and the serum would cause immediate chemical damage to the inner ear's exposed neural tissue, especially with any perforation of the eardrum.

**The Lunar fraction is the appropriate fraction for delicate mucous membranes.**

The Lunar — the pure middle-water cut of the distillation — is a fundamentally different substance. At approximately 133 USCM, near-neutral ORP, and a mild pH of 9.4–9.7, it is essentially hyper-purified water: stripped of the ammonia volatiles that make the Solar fraction caustic, and stripped of the heavy mineral concentration that makes the Saturn serum osmotically aggressive. It is the fraction closest in character to deuterium-depleted water — light, structurally clean, and gentle on sensitive tissue.

## The Lunar Fraction — Eyes and Ears

In this practitioner's experience, the Lunar fraction has been in consistent daily use as eye and ear drops.

**Eyes:** A few drops of the Lunar fraction applied directly to the eye. The mild alkalinity — well within the range that ocular tissue tolerates — is soothing rather than caustic. The ultra-low conductivity means the drop carries almost no dissolved load, making it non-irritating and easily absorbed. The eyes respond well: the sensation is cooling and clean.

**Ears:** A few drops warmed slightly and applied to the ear canal. Pure, warm water is the standard recommendation for gentle ear hygiene; the Lunar fraction is functionally this, with the structural properties of triple-distilled biological water rather than tap or filtered water.

The Lunar fraction for these applications should be fresh from a clean run and stored sealed in dark glass. The same fraction should not be left open or contaminated — it carries very little mineral load and no preservative chemistry, so hygiene in storage and application matters.

**Raw fluid** — fresh, unconcentrated, isotonic — is what the broader traditional practice describes for mucous membrane application. The Lunar fraction occupies a middle position: it is distilled and therefore stripped of the original fluid's full mineral and biological complexity, but it is also stripped of the concentration that makes the Solar and Saturn fractions inappropriate for these tissues. Its simplicity is what makes it suitable here.

## Storage

**Solar fraction (spirit):**
Store in dark amber or cobalt glass with an airtight seal — a polycone cap or PTFE-lined lid. The volatile ammonia will escape through any imperfect seal and the fraction will lose its alkaline potency. Keep cool and dark. Do not store near other liquids that could be mistaken for it.

**Saturn serum (liquid reduction):**
Store in a wide-mouth amber glass jar with a tight-fitting lid. The serum is hygroscopic — in coastal or humid environments, an open jar will begin pulling moisture from the air immediately, diluting the concentration. Use a small dedicated spoon to remove what is needed; do not introduce a wet toothbrush or wet fingers into the master jar.
