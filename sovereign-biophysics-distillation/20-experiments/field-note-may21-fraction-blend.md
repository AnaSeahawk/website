# Field Note — May 21 2026
## Fraction Reunion: Lunar Essence Blended into Saturn Serum

---

Running low. Only a small dropper bottle of Lunar essence remaining. The decision was practical: blend the remaining essence into the Saturn serum rather than continue applying either at full strength separately. The serum is being used as a body preparation — a lotion — and the essence is being added to it in small amounts, diluting in.

The blend feels better than full-strength application.

This is worth sitting with. The previous practice was generous with the Solar spirit and Lunar essence — spraying full-strength onto the body in volume. This was possible when the stores were abundant, and there was no felt reason to restrict. But "extravagant" is the right word looking back. The volume was large and the concentration was high, and the body received it that way: a high signal at high volume.

The diluted blend — Lunar essence in Saturn serum carrier — delivers a lower concentration of the volatile fraction within the mineral-dense base. And the body's response is different: more gradual, more integrated, more present. *More realistic* is the phrase that comes. As though the full-strength application was in some register too much to fully receive, and the diluted blend sits within a range the body can read more slowly and completely.

The three fractions have now been partially reunited in practice. The Solar spirit (the remaining dropper bottle), which is not in this blend, is being conserved. The Lunar essence is in the Saturn serum carrier. Two of the three fractions meeting.

The alchemical formula for the completion of the work is the reunion of all separated fractions — *solve et coagula*, the spirit and essence returning to the fixed retentate. What is happening in this blend is not the full completion but a step toward it: the Lunar essence (the clarified middle fraction, the nectar) returning to the Saturn serum (the mineral-dense retentate, the fixed body of the distillation). The body receiving this blend is receiving both the refined volatile and the concentrated mineral intelligence together, as the body originally produced them together before distillation separated them.

The feeling of preciousness that comes with scarcity: something changed in the relationship to the material when there was less of it. The large stores permitted a kind of unconsidered generosity — using freely because freely held. Now each drop is attended to. The practice has become more conscious at the moment when the material itself is almost gone. This may not be coincidental. Scarcity focuses attention. The medicine working at lower concentration, with more awareness applied to it, may be more effective than the same medicine at higher concentration applied without full presence.

*Note for future practice: consider formulating deliberately as a diluted blend from the start rather than applying fractions at full strength. The Saturn serum as carrier for the Lunar essence is a complete preparation — mineral base plus refined volatile fraction — that maps onto classical formulation principles (the concentrated substance in a bioavailable carrier) and produces a different, more integrated body response than full-strength single-fraction application.*
