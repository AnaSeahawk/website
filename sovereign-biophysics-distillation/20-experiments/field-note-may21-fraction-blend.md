# Field Note — May 21 2026
## Fraction Reunion: Lunar Essence Blended into Saturn Serum

---

Running low on Saturn serum — only a small amount remaining, with no aged plasma available to distil more. Fresh Lunar essence is still in good supply. The decision was practical: blend the remaining serum drops into the Lunar essence rather than continue applying either separately. The essence becomes the body lotion base, and the serum is extended through it.

The blend feels better than full-strength single-fraction application.

This is worth sitting with. The previous practice used the Solar spirit and Lunar essence freely — spraying full-strength onto the body in volume. Abundant stores made generosity unconsidered. *Extravagant* is the right word looking back: high concentration, high volume, the body receiving a strong signal all at once.

The blend is different. The Lunar essence carries the Saturn serum — the fluid volatile fraction delivering the mineral-dense retentate into the skin. The serum provides what it always has: the body's concentrated fixed intelligence, the mineral archive of the distillation. The essence provides the delivery — a bioavailable fluid carrier that takes that mineral density in rather than presenting it as a thick residue. The body's response is more gradual, more integrated, more present. *More realistic* is the phrase that comes. As though the previous full-strength application was registering too fast or too intensely to be fully received, and the blend sits within a range the body can read more slowly and completely.

The three fractions have now been partially reunited in practice. The Solar spirit (conserved in a small dropper bottle) is not in this blend. The Saturn serum has been folded into the Lunar essence. Two of the three fractions meeting: the fixed mineral retentate dissolved into the clarified volatile carrier.

This is *solve et coagula* in topical practice — not as concept but as direct experience. The separation the distillation performed, the reunion the blend makes. The body receiving the two fractions together, as they were before the still separated them, at the concentration and ratio that emerged from running low rather than from deliberate formulation design.

The feeling of preciousness that comes with scarcity: something changed in the relationship to the material when there was almost none left. The large stores permitted a kind of unconsidered generosity. Now each drop of serum is attended to. The practice has become more conscious precisely at the moment the material is almost gone. Scarcity focuses attention. The medicine at lower concentration, with full presence applied to it, may be more effective than the same medicine at higher concentration applied without thought.

*Note for future practice: the Lunar essence as carrier for Saturn serum is a complete preparation — the fluid volatile fraction delivering the concentrated mineral intelligence of the retentate. Consider formulating deliberately in this proportion from the start: serum as the precious, concentrated active; essence as the vehicle. Maps onto classical anupana principles — the medicine in a carrier designed for its depth of delivery.*
