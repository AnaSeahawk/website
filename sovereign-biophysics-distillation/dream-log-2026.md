## May 20, 2026

The intensity has become normal. Not dramatic — just the new baseline. Going to bed now involves a mental preparation: opening to what comes without interfering. The old lucid dream instructions (find your hands, look for a sign, try to remember) are set aside for now. What's being practiced instead is awareness without the grasping reflex. Being present in the dream without announcing the presence loudly enough to wake up.

The edge is: awareness without waking because of it. Learning to sit right there without tipping either direction. The knowing as ground rather than a thing being noticed.

Relaxing into observation. Not trying to see the lesson. Not trying to hold it. Just open.
