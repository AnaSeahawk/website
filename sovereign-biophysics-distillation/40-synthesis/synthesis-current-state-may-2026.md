## I. What Has Been Confirmed

### The Three-Fraction Structure Holds

The February 2026 run established the baseline. Repeated distillation since then has confirmed the three-fraction structure:

- **Solar spirit** (first volatile fraction): strongly alkaline (pH 9.9–10.09), electron-donating (ORP -37 mV), sharp character on tasting, fast rise under low heat. The charge inversion from the fresh baseline (+77 mV) to the Solar spirit (-37 mV) is the most reproducible measurement in the archive.
- **Lunar essence** (bulk aqueous distillate): lighter, cleaner, slight alkalinity, low ORP — mineral-depleted but electrochemically distinct from neutral distilled water.
- **Saturn retentate** (fixed mineral fraction): dense, hygroscopic on a zero-NaCl dietary baseline, workable as a topical preparation. The absence of sodium chloride from the diet is visibly confirmed in the retentate — no aggressive crystallisation, smooth mineral matrix.

### The Dietary Baseline Is Doing Measurable Work

The Saturn retentate's composition is directly readable as diet. On a zero-NaCl, dairy-ghee-grain baseline, the dominant electrolytes are potassium salts rather than sodium chloride — confirmed by the retentate's hygroscopic behaviour (potassium phosphate and potassium citrate absorb moisture; NaCl crystallises). This is not a small theoretical point. It means the body's dietary choices show up directly in the distillate, in a form that can be observed without laboratory analysis.

The Solar spirit's effect on grain — alkaline hydrolysis producing nixtamalization-adjacent breakdown, most effectively in a distilled water soak — is a functional result that has been documented and can be reproduced. This is one of the archive's cleaner practical observations: a measurable effect, a proposed mechanism (ammonia-driven alkaline hydrolysis), and a consistent outcome across preparations.

## II. The Ayurvedic Frame: What It Adds

The agni/dhatvagni/ojas framework, developed in the May 2026 orientation documents, does something the earlier biophysical framing could not: it gives the dietary simplification process a mechanism that isn't purely eliminative.

The dhatvagni chain — seven tissue fires operating in sequence from rasa (plasma) through shukra/artava (reproductive tissue) to ojas — explains why the dietary transition takes years rather than weeks. Each dhatvagni must be functioning at sama (balanced) for the chain to complete and produce ojas at its end. Disrupted fires downstream of any dietary interference break the sequence. The practitioner isn't just removing substances. They are restoring the conditions under which the body's own refinement chain can complete its work.

The four states of agni (sama, vishama, tikshna, manda) provide a diagnostic vocabulary that the archive has been reaching for without having had the language. The distinction that matters most for this practice: a body with manda agni eating less is not the same condition as a body with sama agni eating less. The former is pathological; the latter is a signal that the chain is completing at a finer level of input. Without the agni framework, this distinction is invisible — and practitioners in both states look, from the outside, like they're doing the same thing.

## V. What Remains Unmeasured

The archive is explicit about this. Listing the gaps here as a single reference:

1. **No GC/MS data** on the Solar or Lunar fractions. Volatile composition is inferred from biochemistry, not directly measured.
2. **No 24-hour urinalysis** for the practitioner. Starting-material composition is reasoned from known dietary inputs, not measured.
3. **No controlled characterisation of the aging process** — microbial community, pH trajectory, fermentation endpoint. Duration is tracked; chemistry is not.
4. **The distilled water input is uncharacterised** beyond observable properties (visual clarity, distinct surface behaviour). If it is meaningfully structured or deuterium-depleted, it is an active variable in the plasma.
5. **The microscopy findings are preliminary** — not yet cross-referenced with duration of practice, not yet examined with SEM, not yet replicated across a larger sample.
6. **The clearing-phase plasma** is a distinct material from stable-baseline plasma. The archive has data from both conditions but they are not yet cleanly separated in the experimental record.

None of these gaps are fatal to the work. They are the honest boundary of what has been measured versus what has been inferred.
