# Plasma

## Working Definition

In this archive, *plasma* is a working term for the body's living carrier fluid as it appears inside the practice, the field record, and the symbolic-philosophical language of Sovereign Biophysics.

It does not automatically mean blood plasma. It should not be read as a standard biomedical claim unless the context explicitly says so.

## Active Registers

- **Field:** the collected living substrate under observation
- **Instrument Table:** a sample with measurable properties such as pH, TDS, ORP, conductivity, scent, appearance, and fraction behaviour
- **Interpretation Chamber:** the body-produced carrier of charge, history, and condition
- **Mythic Field:** prima materia, living water, source substance
- **Philosophical Ground:** the body's own production treated as meaningful rather than discarded

## Guardrail

When the archive speaks in an external biomedical register, the term should be translated or qualified so it is not confused with blood plasma or presented as a universal scientific category.
