# Field

## Working Definition

*Field* names the living context in which observation happens.

It may refer to direct experience, environment, relational atmosphere, or the archive's wider observational ecology.

## Active Registers

- **Field:** the immediate lived event
- **Pattern Room:** the context that repeats around an observation
- **Interpretation Chamber:** the relational setting that gives an event meaning
- **Philosophical Ground:** reality understood as relational rather than isolated

## Guardrail

The word *field* does not automatically mean electromagnetic field, biofield, or a measurable force.

If a technical field is meant, the measurement method or source should be named.
