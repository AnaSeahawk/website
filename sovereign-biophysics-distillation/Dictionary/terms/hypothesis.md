# Hypothesis

## Working Definition

A *hypothesis* is a provisional explanation.

It proposes a possible relationship or mechanism without claiming finality.

## Role in the Archive

Hypothesis language belongs in the Hypothesis Forge. It may arise from field observation, measurement, repeated pattern, comparative archive work, or external research.

Its value is not certainty. Its value is that it gives future observation something clear to test, refine, or release.

## Guardrail

A hypothesis should remain visibly provisional.

It should not be written as proof, prescription, doctrine, or settled mechanism.
