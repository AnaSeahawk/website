# Where to Begin

---

The body is the first door.

This archive documents one practitioner's return to the body's own living waters: what the body produces, what those productions carry, what distillation reveals, and how the old traditions begin to read differently when the jar is in the hand.

There are measurements here. There are myths. There are histories, hypotheses, method notes, field records, dreams, and questions still forming. They do not all speak the same language. They are kept in relationship without being made to impersonate one another.

The simplest way in is to walk the living path first.

---

## The Living Path

The main sequence is the archive's spine. It begins with recognition and moves toward the current state of the work.

1. [Manifesto of the Clear Mirror](manifesto-clear-mirror.md) — the vow and the threshold.
2. [The First Water](the-first-water.md) — the body knew this loop before language.
3. [Women of Alchemy](women-of-alchemy.md) — the lineage, the burning, and the feeling of not being alone.
4. [The Jar Held to the Light](the-jar-held-to-the-light.md) — the first recognition when the clear water came through.
5. [Shivambu](../10-method/shivambu.md) — the name, the relationship, and the social reality of beginning.
6. [The Living Loop](../10-method/the-living-loop.md) — four years of practice as daily life.
7. [Agni](agni.md) — the fire that knows how transformation completes.
8. [All the Practice Is for the Medicine](all-the-practice-is-for-the-medicine.md) — the thesis of what the practices are for.
9. [The Three Waters](the-three-waters.md) — Solar, Lunar, and Saturn as the three voices the still reveals.
10. [Three Years of Topical Practice](../40-synthesis/synthesis-three-year-topical-observations.md) — what has been observed across years.
11. [Current State of the Work](../40-synthesis/synthesis-current-state-may-2026.md) — what is known, what is open, and where the practice is moving.

This path does not require agreement. It asks only that each page be read in its own register: personal record as personal record, measurement as measurement, myth as myth, hypothesis as hypothesis.

## The Rooms Off the Path

The rest of the archive can be entered when the question arises.

**The measured record** holds the experiment reports, meter readings, conditions, and run behaviour. Start with [Ana's First Distillation](../20-experiments/experiment-report-ana-distillation-feb22.md), or move into the current June series through the experiment index.

**The working material** holds practical vocabulary: [Plasma Forms Taxonomy](../10-method/plasma-forms-taxonomy.md), [Distilled Fraction Protocols](../10-method/distilled-fraction-protocols.md), [Observation Protocol](../10-method/observation-protocol-handout.md), and the [Measurement Legend](../Dictionary/measurement-legend.md). These are reference rooms, not the first doorway.

**The body chemistry room** holds the denser biochemical notes: [Hypothesising What Is in the Plasma](../30-analysis/analysis-plasma-composition-hypothesis.md) and [The Biochemistry of the Distillation Fractions](../30-analysis/analysis-fraction-biochemistry.md). These pages answer the question "what might be in it?" without becoming the authority over the experience.

**The mythic and cosmological ground** holds Inanna, Venus, Prabhuta, the moving sky, and the larger symbolic field. These pages keep the old languages available without making them laboratory proof.

**What was done to this knowledge** holds the suppression, anatomical, linguistic, and institutional critique: [The Language of Erasure](the-language-of-erasure.md), [The Map and the Cut](the-map-and-the-cut.md), [The Mother Wound](the-mother-wound.md), and the pharmacological analysis room.

**The key and legend** live in the [Dictionary](../Dictionary/README.md). Use it when a term, meter reading, claim tier, or tradition register needs a stable landing place.

## Public and Private Layers

This public archive holds orientation, bounded method notes, measurements, analysis, synthesis, and vocabulary.

Some records need relationship before they can be read. Intimate operational notes, mucosal pathways, sexuality, partnered practice, and relational field observations belong in the private Vessel layer.

Private does not mean lesser. It means protected.

The public archive can point toward the private layer. It does not expose what requires context, consent, and care.

---

## If The Archive Moves You

The work is not a course and not a programme. There is no promised outcome.

There is a living conversation around it.

If something in the archive has moved from curiosity into the wish for contact, the doorway is [Private Vessel Access](../../The-Vessel/portal.md): one-on-one calls, Mother Spirit, and the current community channel.

The archive is the record.

The community is where the questions can breathe.
