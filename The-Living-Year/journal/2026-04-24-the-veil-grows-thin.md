# The Veil Grows Thin

*April 24–26, 2026 — Sarandë, Albania*

*Single-practitioner record. Personal observation only.*

---

Something has grown thin. The membrane between the waking body and everything beneath it — thinner now than I can remember it being. Not fragile. More like it has become permeable in the way good skin is permeable. Receiving. Present to what moves through.

It begins in the root.

The Lunar essence as a yoni infusion for the first time — a warmth that was also cooling, a quality *ojas* carries in the texts and the body knows before the mind finds the word for it. Something laid down quietly in the deepest tissue. Not corrective. More like the body being given what it recognised and had been waiting for.

I didn't keep it in long. I could have.

When I got up the gravity of the fluid was different. I collected what came out. Skim milk white — the essence itself is clear, so the white was the body's own offering back. Sweet. I gave some to Olivier. He said it tasted like me. I drank the rest.

---

When sleep came it was not a descent. It was a continuation.

The first night — above open water in a storm, pulled through the air like a water skier, two enormous vessels to thread between at speed. Heart racing and completely still at the same time. The body moving through something the mind had no language for and didn't need any. And then the storm calling for rest — a massive blanket of colour unrolling itself, wrapping around me, stopping exactly at the threshold of the neck. Refuge offered without asking.

The second night — a man with a silver breastplate. Multi-coloured shirt beneath it, long painted nails the same colours, shoulder-length hair, beautiful skin. He was doing something with his hands — fluid, trance-like, his face part of it too. A silk ribbon quality. Continuous. Impossible to trace back step by step.

The breastplate should have reflected everything. It reflected nothing — only pure light. A surface so refined it had stopped performing the function of a mirror. No image. No return of the viewer to themselves. Only luminosity.

I knew I was in the trance while I was in it. When he finished I told him he had hypnotised me. He hadn't captured anything. It was a transmission — showing me a current that was already mine, moving through hands I already have.

I woke and walked straight to the mat on the veranda. No pause. The body already knew. Ballet-like movement through the neck and shoulders, everything that had been held releasing into the morning air.

---

When dreams arrive this vivid I look at what's happening in the sky afterwards — not to explain but to feel the confirmation.

Sun in Taurus square Pluto in Aquarius was exact. The body, the senses, the material world under alchemical pressure. I walked through streets built entirely around food and felt nothing but curiosity — the way you observe a place you once lived and no longer do. Not resistance. Not will. The charge has simply changed. The square had already resolved itself in the body long before the sky thought to ask.

Uranus entering Gemini — electricity moving into the hands. The nervous system. The man's silk ribbon hands in the dream. The needle threaded at speed with the mind gone.

Venus into Gemini on the 24th, the day of the infusion — full-spectrum colour and magnetic aliveness. The blanket. The shirt. The nails. All of it luminous.

Moon in Leo through both nights — silver and receptive, presiding over the chest and the vital centre. The breastplate that held only light. *Ojas* made visible in the dream: cool, white, sovereign — a surface that had stopped needing to give anything back.

The sky was not above me. It was moving through me. The dreams brought more meaning into the experience than I could have brought to them. That is always how it goes when something real has happened in the body.
