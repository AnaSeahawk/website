# Relationship

*Plants, bodies, and the intelligence of use.*

This folder holds the plant relationship entries and the foundational framework for how they are approached. Each plant is met as a living presence — not as a compound or a protocol, but as something the body encounters under specific conditions and through specific preparation.

The framework is Āyurvedic in method, not in label. Plants that do not appear in classical Saṃhitās are not called Āyurvedic — they are approached Āyurvedically, through rasa, guṇa, vīrya, vipāka, and karma. The distinction is held throughout.
